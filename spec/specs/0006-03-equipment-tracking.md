# 0006-03 Equipment Tracking Specification

## Status
Complete

## Overview
This document specifies the requirements for recording and tracking farm equipment details, based on Feature 3 of PRD 0006.

## Requirements

1. **General Equipment Tracking**
   - Record details of all farm equipment used for application.

2. **LESSE Tracking**
   - Specifically focus on Low Emission Sward Spreading Equipment (LESSE).
   - Allow selection of equipment types such as:
     - Dribble bars
     - Trailing shoes
     - Soil injection systems

3. **Exceptions Recording**
   - Provide a mechanism to record equipment exceptions, such as the use of inverted splash plates.
   - Require users to provide justification for the exception (e.g., impractical due to field slope).
