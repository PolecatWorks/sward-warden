# 0007-03 General Farm Records Export Specification

## Status
Open

## Overview
This document specifies the requirements for generating and exporting general farm records, based on Feature 3 of PRD 0007.

## Requirements

1. **Export Functionality**
   - Provide a feature to export comprehensive general farm records.
   - Support retention schedules (e.g., must be prepared by 30 June each year and retained for five years).

2. **Record Details**
   - The export must include at least the following details:
     - Agricultural area
     - Livestock numbers
     - Cropping regimes
     - Field sizes and locations
