# 0007-02 Annual Fertilisation Accounts Specification

## Status
Complete

## Overview
This document outlines the requirements for generating annual fertilisation accounts, based on Feature 2 of PRD 0007.

## Requirements

1. **Summary Generation**
   - Provide functionality to generate a comprehensive annual fertilisation account summary.

2. **Derogation Support**
   - For farms operating under a derogation, format the generated account specifically for online submission to the NIEA.
   - Submissions must be valid for the previous calendar year and submitted by the 1 March deadline.
