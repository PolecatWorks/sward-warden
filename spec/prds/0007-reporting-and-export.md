# PRD 0007: Reporting and Export

## Overview
This document specifies the requirements for generating reports and exporting data required by authorities such as DAERA and NIEA to ensure regulatory compliance under the Farm Sustainability Standards (FSS) and Nutrients Action Programme (NAP).

## Key Requirements

1. **Digital Pesticide Records Export**
   - Provide the ability to export Plant Protection Product (PPP) application records in a "machine-readable" digital format suitable for electronic submission to DAERA.
   - Ensure the export includes the required MAPP (Product authorization number), EPPO crop/land-use code, and BBCH growth-stage code for each record.

2. **Annual Fertilisation Accounts**
   - Generate an annual fertilisation account summary.
   - For farms operating under a derogation, format the account for online submission to the NIEA by 1 March for the previous calendar year.

3. **General Farm Records Export**
   - Provide an export of general farm records that must be prepared by 30 June each year and retained for five years.
   - Include details on the agricultural area, livestock numbers, cropping regimes, and field sizes/locations.

4. **Soil Analysis Reports**
   - Generate reports demonstrating crop requirements based on available soil test results, necessary for proving the need for P-rich organic manures or chemical phosphorus fertilisers.

5. **Import/Export Reporting**
   - Generate export records of sward/manure movements to be submitted to NIEA annually (by 31 January, or 1 March for derogated farms).

## FE Requirements
- **Report Preview**: Users must be able to preview reports in a high-fidelity, clean layout before exporting.
- **Export Progress**: Provide clear, premium visual feedback during the data generation and download process.
- **Compliance Status Dashboard**: A "Reporting Center" view should use the Bento grid pattern to show upcoming deadlines and the status of various mandatory reports.
